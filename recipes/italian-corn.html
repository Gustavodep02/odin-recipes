Irresistible Italian Corn

This is a delicious seasoned corn side dish. DELICIOUS. Must TRY NOW!!


  • 1 ½ tablespoons butter
  • 2 ½ cups whole kernel sweet corn
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over high heat. Heat butter in hot skillet until almost completely melted;
    stir in corn. Season corn with Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
    Cook seasoned corn until light golden brown, about 5 minutes.
